Engineering Degree

Overview of the Degree

This degree aims to train professionals with a solid theoretical and practical foundation in engineering. Throughout the program, students will acquire fundamental knowledge in mathematics, computer science, electrical engineering, and more.

Degree Structure

The degree program is divided into 4 years. Below is an overview of the years and the corresponding subjects:

Year Subject Category Description
1st Year Mathematics I Mathematics Fundamentals of calculus and linear algebra, essential for engineering.
1st Year Physics I Basic Sciences Study of basic physics principles, such as mechanics and thermodynamics.
2nd Year Programming I Computer Science Introduction to basic programming concepts and data structures.
2nd Year Fundamentals of Computational Engineering Computer Science Exploration of the fundamental principles of computational engineering. More information.
3rd Year Computer Networks Computer Science Study of computer networks, protocols, and communication architecture.
3rd Year Electronics I Electronics Fundamentals of electronic circuits and their application in engineering devices.
4th Year Engineering Projects Practical Applications Development and management of engineering projects integrating the knowledge acquired.
4th Year Final Degree Project Practical Applications Preparation of a final project demonstrating the knowledge acquired during the degree program.
